Area having a common outlet for its surface runoff. [<a href="https://library.wmo.int/doc_num.php?explnum_id=8209"><font color="#0000ff"><u>WMO-No.385</u></font></a>] A catchment defines a fundamental subdivision of the landscape in which water can be modelled as draining to a single outlet, to an adjacent waterbody, or internally to an area devoid of waterbody features. Four types of elementary catchments are recognized: reach catchments, bank catchments, empty catchments, and water catchments.
