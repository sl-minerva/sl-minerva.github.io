A derived linear feature that realizes a catchment specifically as a path connecting the inflow or headwater start point with the outflow of the catchment. The direction of a flowpath is always downstream. [<a href="https://github.com/NRCan/chyf/blob/master/docs/chyfConceptualModel.md#flowpath"><font color="#0000ff"><u>CHyF</u></font></a>]<br/><br/>
