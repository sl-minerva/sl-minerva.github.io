“Any location of hydrologic significance located on a hydrologic network that is a hydrology-specific realization of a hydrologic nexus.” [HY_F] The term “on” is interpreted to mean that a point representing the hydro location is on or near a flowpath. In all cases a hydro(logic) nexus either already exists in the dataset or potentially could be defined, based on projecting the geographic position of the hydro location onto an adjacent flowpath.
