Notes: An elementary catchment consisting entirely of a waterbody of a portion of a waterbody, where the geometry of the feature is a polygon in 2D. A single small lake may be geometrically equivalent to a water catchment. A larger lake or a river sufficiently large to have polygonal geometry may be broken into a series of areas, each defined as a water catchment.<br/><br/>A water catchment participates in a catchment network. The attributes of a water catchment differ from those of a waterbody with areal extent. Water catchments are represented directly in a hygraph data structure, whereas waterbodies are not.<br/>
